World of Warcraft Druid Specializations- An in-depth review

Guardian

Kind of boring. You get to be a bear which is neat but also kind of lame because who wants to be a bear?

Feral

Kind of bad. You're just a worse rogue. You're also a cat which is boring, who wants to be a cat?

Restoration

The best. You get to be a tree. Who doesn't want to be a tree?

Balance

Bad right now but also cool. Usually overpowered. You get to be a giant chicken thing that shoots lasers. Pretty cool?
